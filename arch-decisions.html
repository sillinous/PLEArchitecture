ADR-007

Pyramid of Prosperity Layer Definitions Adopted

Jan 2026 Conceptual

Decision

  • Layer 1 - UBI: Universal unconditional floor (~$12K/year)
  • Layer 2 - Public Assets: Healthcare, education, infrastructure
  • Layer 3 - Collective Ownership: Sovereign wealth dividends
  • Layer 4 - Individual Assets: Personal investments
  • Layer 5 - Residual Wages: Voluntary market labor
