PROP-015

Data Dividends Integration

Description

Proposal to add "Data Dividends" as a component of Layer 3 (Collective Ownership) in the Pyramid of Prosperity. This would establish a mechanism for citizens to receive compensation for the use of their personal data by corporations.

Context

As corporations increasingly monetize user data, citizens should receive direct compensation for their contributions to this value creation. This aligns with our principles of collective ownership and human agency.
