# Post-Labor Economics — Brand DNA

## Brand Essence

**One Word:** Liberation

**Tagline:** "Prosperity Beyond Work"

**Elevator Pitch:** Post-Labor Economics is a movement building the intellectual and policy frameworks for a world where human dignity and economic security are no longer dependent on selling your time. We're not preparing for job loss—we're preparing for liberation.

---

## Brand Story

### The Narrative Arc

We stand at an inflection point in human history. For millennia, survival meant labor. Your worth was your work. But automation, AI, and technological abundance are severing that ancient link between toil and sustenance.

This isn't a crisis—it's an opportunity. The crisis is clinging to outdated systems while the world transforms beneath us.

Post-Labor Economics doesn't fear this future. We're architecting it. Through rigorous research, bold policy, and community power, we're building bridges from the economy of scarcity to the economy of abundance.

**We are the cartographers of liberation.**

### Origin Symbol: L/0

The **L/0** mark represents the transition point—from **L**abor to **Zero** (post-labor). It's a threshold, a phase shift, a moment of transformation. The forward slash isn't division; it's a pathway. It's the bridge we're building together.

## Brand Applications

### Logo Usage

The L/0 mark should:
- Always have clear space equal to the height of the "0"
- Appear in Horizon (#1B4D3E) on light backgrounds
- Appear in white or Dawn (#F4A261) on dark backgrounds
- Never be distorted, rotated beyond 15°, or placed on busy backgrounds

### Pattern Language

**The Threshold Pattern:** A subtle diagonal gradient or line suggesting transition, the "/" in our L/0 mark. Used as backgrounds, dividers, hover states.

**The Grid of Possibility:** Structured layouts with organic breaks—grids that breathe, not confine.

**Warmth Touches:** Subtle grain textures, rounded corners, generous whitespace—human, not sterile.

## Brand Don'ts

❌ Don't use fear-based messaging about job loss
❌ Don't position against workers or employment
❌ Don't use cold, clinical tech aesthetics
❌ Don't talk down to audiences
❌ Don't use jargon without explanation
❌ Don't make promises without evidence
❌ Don't use stock photos of robots replacing humans

---

## Brand Do's

✅ Lead with possibility and human dignity
✅ Ground bold visions in rigorous research
✅ Make complex ideas genuinely accessible
✅ Center human stories alongside data
✅ Build bridges across political divides
✅ Invite participation, don't lecture
✅ Celebrate progress while acknowledging challenges
