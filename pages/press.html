Key Messages

The Core Thesis

AI, automation, and robotics will eliminate obligatory human labor within our lifetimes. This isn't about banning work—it's about making work voluntary rather than coerced. We need concrete frameworks now to ensure this transition benefits everyone.

The Economic Challenge

When jobs disappear, so does the mechanism by which most people receive income. The Pyramid of Prosperity provides a five-layer framework for distributing income through ownership rather than wages—using proven mechanisms like sovereign wealth funds, cooperatives, and universal services.

The Democratic Challenge

Labor power has been the primary source of citizen leverage throughout history. The Pyramid of Power shows how algorithmic systems—blockchain identity, open payment rails, transparent governance—can replace labor power with new forms of democratic accountability.

The Coalition

Exhausted workers and forward-thinking capitalists both want to end obligatory labor—for different reasons. Building bridges between these groups creates a coalition that can overcome the ideological resistance to change.
