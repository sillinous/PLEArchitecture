About This Template

The Standard Proposal template is designed for general-purpose changes to the PLE architecture. It provides a comprehensive structure that ensures all necessary information is captured for community review.

When to Use

  • Proposing new components or concepts
  • Suggesting modifications to existing artifacts
  • Process improvements
  • Community governance changes

Tips

  • Keep the summary under 100 words
  • Be specific about impact on stakeholders
  • Include concrete implementation steps
  • List open questions for community discussion
