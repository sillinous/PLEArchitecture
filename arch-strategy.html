Core Strategies 5 Strategies

The approaches we use to achieve our goals. These are the "how" that connects goals to tactics.

Content-First Growth

Lead with high-quality educational content. Build audience through value, not marketing. Free newsletter, YouTube videos, and open-source frameworks.

Awareness Community Trust

Unlikely Alliances

Bridge traditionally adversarial groups by finding shared interests. Exhausted workers AND forward-thinking capitalists. Labor unions AND tech executives.

Coalition Policy Reach

Evidence-Based Advocacy

Ground every proposal in research and data. Reference real-world examples: Alaska Permanent Fund, Norway's sovereign wealth, UBI pilots. Update frameworks when evidence contradicts assumptions.

Research Credibility Policy

Open-Source Architecture

Make all frameworks freely available under CC BY-SA. Invite contribution and critique. The architecture belongs to everyone.

Frameworks Community Adoption

Distributed Organizing

Empower local chapters and regional advocates. Provide tools and frameworks, not centralized control. Scale through multiplication, not expansion.
